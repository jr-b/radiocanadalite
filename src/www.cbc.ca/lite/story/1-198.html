2022 Black Changemakers series now accepting nominations

CBC Communications | Posted: Monday, September 27, 2021, 10:00 AM UTC | Last Updated: September 27

We're looking for people making a difference in their communities

Image | Miscellaneous

Caption: CBC Quebec is back with our second annual Black Changemakers series. We are looking for current-day Changemakers from our Black communities who are helping to shape our future. Deadline for nominations is Nov. 5. (Black Changemakers - Season 2)

CBC Quebec is back with our second annual Black Changemakers series. We are looking for current-day Changemakers from our Black communities who are helping to shape our future.

This project highlights Black individuals whose efforts to make a change are bringing about a meaningful impact and inspiring others across the province.

We invite you to nominate your colleagues, friends and family — anyone who is making a difference, big or small in your community.

Tell us about a person who deserves the spotlight. CBC Quebec is accepting nominations until Nov. 5 at 11:59 p.m. ET

     

  • Meet Quebec's 2021 Black Changemakers

    •  

Submissions will be reviewed by a panel comprised of community advisors, CBC journalists and past Black Changemakers finalists.

Who we're looking for

Nominees can be artists, creators, health-care workers, educators, entrepreneurs, community organizers and everyone in between from cities across the province.

How to submit

     

  • Fill out the form below. 

    •  

  • Include a paragraph about why you believe this person (or you, if you are self-nominating) should be included.

    •  

In the month of February, we will highlight and celebrate the finalists with coverage across all our platforms through radio and TV interviews, articles and more.

Have questions? Email us at blackchangemakersqc@cbc.ca

P.E.I. holding COVID-19 pandemic briefing

CBC News | Posted: Tuesday, September 28, 2021, 1:44 PM UTC | Last Updated: Just now

Watch the briefing live here

Media | Miscellaneous

Caption: P.E.I.'s Chief Public Health Officer provides an update on COVID-19 in the province.

P.E.I. Chief Public Health Officer Dr. Heather Morrison is giving a  briefing on the COVID-19 pandemic.

The briefing comes as concerns rise about the fourth wave of the pandemic. This month the province has seen more active cases than it ever has.

The province is working on plans to introduce the P.E.I. Vax Pass next week, which will require that people be fully vaccinated to access many public spaces. It is also introducing new rules for testing for people arriving from New Brunswick.

  • Questions remain as P.E.I. Vax Pass date approaches
  • Ferry traffic expected to increase as N.B. border rules tighten

The province currently has 40 active cases of COVID-19, and has seen a total of 294.
